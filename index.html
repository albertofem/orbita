Make music, from the planetary orbits.

Orbita is a fun and easy music making app which turns planetary movements into musical harmonies. You can create dreamy and fantastic sounds with only tapping the screen of your mobile device. In spite of its simplicity and intuitiveness, it gives you a great variety and possibility of music.

Let's explore the universe of music, with Orbita!
